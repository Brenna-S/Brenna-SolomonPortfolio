Who Is Brenna ?


Hi my name is Brenna Solomon a 22 year old Software Engineer. I have obtained an associates degree in Business Administration and now a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science. I have always been very fascinated with the technology world ever since I was a kid. Whether I was taking computers apart and putting them back together, or replacing parts, or even designing my own personal projects, the love and passion for this field started at what feels like the beginning of time.

I have managed to maneuver life as a first generation American living in a single parent immigrant household. The drive and motivation I have for success is unmatched. I am a self-taught nail technican devoted to creating a safe space for the women of the community to feel their best self. I have developed and molded my business for the past 3 years and I'm excited for the journey that comes.



I possess many skills beneficial to any team. I am very hard working, goal oriented, fast learner, team player, and so much more. I am planning on pursuing a Masters degree next and work on some freelance projects. Stay connected with me to get the inside scoop on what comes next in my career!
